ğŸ“ˆ Key Business Insights

Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn rates.

Churn risk is highest during the early customer lifecycle stages.

Long-tenure customers contribute substantial revenue loss despite lower churn frequency.

Payment methods and service types strongly influence customer retention behavior.

Customer value increases as tenure increases, highlighting the importance of early engagement strategies.
